Custom Electronics

This knowledge and passion has grown thru the years and has been augmented by my degree in Computer Science -- Network Administration, as well as 6+ years of experience designing, configuring, and maintaining networks. Coding

I got my start in coding when I was introduced to an old HP x386. I was told that there wasnt any games or anything, but I was free to check it out. I quickly found out there was a BASIC compiler and a few sample programs...

After spending many days sifting thru outdated and archaic books on BASIC and QUICKBasic, I realized I was thoroughly in love with coding!

Since then I have grown into more sophistocated and modern languages such as python and C++. I am not a software engineer by any means, but I have managed tobuild some impressive apps and have written numerous scripts for automation and system administration.
